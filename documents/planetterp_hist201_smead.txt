Course: HIST201 - Interpreting American History: From 1865 to the Present
Professor: Howard Smead

Review 1:
Very good lecturer, TAs are also very nice and helpful. Exam 1 was difficult, paper and pencil writing 2 essays 5 paragraphs each, however grading was nice. Final exam on online similar format but on the computer.

Review 2:
Easy class but discussion is 30% of your grade. also grading is harsh on assignments and all TA based. Also a really annoyingly long paper due over thanksgiving break.

Review 3:
You don't really need to go to the lectures, but make sure to go to the Friday discussion section and raise your hand a bunch, since participation is really heavily weighted for your final grade. Our midterm was in person and our final was online. Take Chiles if you can because all of Chiles' exams are online. Smead seems super cool and interested in the class. Fall Semseter 2025 doesn't have discussion posts anymore like the semester of spring 2025 did, which made the class easier. TA review sessions are also super helpful.
