Professor: Erin Moody

Review 1:
I really enjoyed her teaching. The way she taught was very engaging. The reason i got a bad grade is lowkey my fault i just wasn't locked in, but I will say all the tests are multiple choice but some questions are like i ii and iii and it says which ones make sense, so its less multiple choice but thats it other than that shes an awesome procgessor shes so upbeat and I wish she would teach marco aswell.

Review 2:
Definitely take with Moody if you can. Her grading structure is much more forgiving compared to other professors. There are 3 exams and a final, but if you do well on the first 3 you don't have to take the final. I was able to pass without ever going to class. She offers a zoom option and you have to do clickers, but other than that everything you need to know is in the textbook and thats what I used to learn/study. She allows a whole 8.5/11 sheet of paper front of back as a cheat sheet for every exam. She also allows 1 retake I believe.

Review 3:
Dr. Moody is a knowledgeable and kind professor, though she occasionally goes on tangents about real-world economic issues that do not appear on the exams. The exams themselves were fairly easy if you took some time to read the textbook, made use of the allowed cheat sheet (I always made mine very detailed), and attended lectures. Personally, I had a background in econ, so this class wasn’t bad, but some found the tests to be hard if they hadn’t taken econ beforehand. The best way to do well is by practicing and attending her office hours. All of the exams were multiple choice and she graded them that same day which was amazing! I would recommend Dr. Moody for this class as her grading policy is super lenient and she seems to care a lot about her students.
